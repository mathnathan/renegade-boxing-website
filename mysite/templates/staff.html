The Owners

The Renegade Boxing Club was created by three friends who share an affinity for health and fitness, and a love for the science of boxing. Their diverse skills compliment one another and have combined to forge a strong and unique business, founded by unbreakable bonds.

  • Joab Corey

The Trainers and Staff

Here at the Renegade Boxing Club we live by a motto "Respect, Honor, and Discipline". To us it's more than a slogan or catch phrase, it outlines our lives and spills over into the gym and our business. We only bring in talented individuals who feel as strongly about this philosophy as we do.

Trainers

Coach Darnell Baker

After beginning boxing in 1968 he managed and trained at gyms all over California including Shields Pittman's Gym, King's Gym, and Gladiator Boxing Club. In Georgia he was involved in the Atlanta Art of Boxing, the Decatur Boxing Club, and opened his own gym, Center Court Boxing Club. Through his career he's worked with many athletes from amateurs to World Champions. He's networked with numerous promoters, managers, and trainers including Harry Kabakoff, Johnny Gant, Byron Ogelsby, Xavier Biggs, and more. He now bring his more than 30 years of boxing experience to us and the FSU Boxing Team.

Coach Kimmus Jones

Coach Jones comes to us from Pensacola, Florida where he was introduced to the science of boxing at an early age by legendary trainer Roy Jones Senior. With almost 20 years of experience competing, training, and participating in the world of boxing, he's been around some of the most talented fighters in the world. Coach Jones is dedicated to our fighters and brings to them a rare skill set and unique perspective. He looks forward to building a foundation with the FSU Boxing Team and in creating a new brand of boxing which will continue to innovate and evolve for years to come.
